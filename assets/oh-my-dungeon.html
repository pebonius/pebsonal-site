introducing Oh my dungeon!

the idea with this one was to be able to make a small roguelike game using one tileset, without having to get additional sprites for like characters and monsters

the tileset has basic dungeon tiles, doors, chests, some chars, mobs and items

thanks to DawnBringer for blessing us all with the db-32 palette

thanks to blurymind for making the tilemap editor i used for the preview image here

also thanks to Hexany for the idea to add gold and treasures
