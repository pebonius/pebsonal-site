legend of hero inn

play in browser

description

this was my entry for the tavern keeper jam

after making high council chair for the chair jam, i wanted to do something less shitposty. there's some stupid jokes, but mostly this is a chill tavern management game.

i joined the jam like a week before deadline and spent most my time adding the patrons, so that this qualified for the jam. if i had more time i'd add a cooking mechanic and a bunch more things. people still liked it so its fine

i added a post-jam build later, with ending for the story and some minor fixes

credits

all assets come from the rpgmaker base rtp
