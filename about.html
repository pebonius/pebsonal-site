about

since 2017 i have been uploading my games to itch under username pebonius. my games are mostly small shitposts created in rpgmaker and renpy for the entertainment of the masses and of myself. i currently resolved to upload jam games to itch, my other games will be uploaded to this here site.

between 2023 and 2024 i have also created a bunch of free pixelart assets, which some people liked. all my assets are available on my itch or OGA account. at some point i'd like to collect them all here. i rarely do pixelart anymore, but if i ever feel like it, i will :)))

i also draw sometimes and post my scribblings over on my bsky. you can find my older drawings on the drawings page. i used to be interested in digital illustration and vg character design a long time ago, but didn't do much about that. now i've become a pro at drawing low effort doodles with a cheap ink pen.

i would like to try other things at some point, such as voice acting or music. if you're making a game and need a really bad voice actor hit me up!
